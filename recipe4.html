BIKNER RASAGULLA

Ingredients:

  • litre milk, full cream
  • lemon juice
  • sugar
  • cup water
  • pod cardamom

Instructions:

  1. Step 1: firstly, in a large vessel get 2 litre milk of milk to a boil stirring occasionally.
  2. Step 2: once the milk comes to a boil, pour 2 tbsp lemon juice and stir well. you can alternatively use curd or vinegar.
  3. Step 3: stir until the milk curdles keeping the flame on low to medium.
  4. Step 4: do not boil further once water separates completely.
  5. Step 5: drain the curdled milk over a cloth-lined over a colander. you can use the leftover water to make soup or knead the dough as they are very nutritious.
  6. Step 6: squeeze off the water completely. be careful as the curdled milk will be very hot.
  7. Step 7: rinse off the curdled milk with fresh water to remove sourness from lemon juice.
  8. Step 8: squeeze off the water completely. do not over squeeze as the moisture in paneer will be lost.
  9. Step 9: hang for 1 hour making sure the water is drained completely, yet remain the moisture.
  10. Step 10: drop in rolled paneer balls one by one into boiling sugar water. enjoy once cooled
