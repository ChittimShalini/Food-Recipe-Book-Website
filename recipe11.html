VEG MANCHURIAN

Ingredients:

  • cabbage
  • spring onion
  • corn flour
  • maida
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • soy sauce
  • chilli sauce
  • vinegar
  • tomato ketchup
  • oil
  • ginger,garlic

Instructions:

  1. Step 1: Take all the veggies, finely chopped, in a bowl along with ginger, garlic, corn flour, maida, salt, black pepper and soy sauce.
  2. Step 2: Mix them well, adding water as required for consistency.
  3. Step 3: Make round balls out of the thick vegetable mixture.
  4. Step 4: Now deep fry the vegetable balls in a pan.
  5. Step 5: Heat some oil in a pan and roast ginger, garlic and green chilli in it for a while
  6. Step 6: Add spring onion, tomato ketchup, chilly sauce, soya sauce and vinegar followed by salt and black pepper.
  7. Step 7: Mix them well and add a mixture of corn flour plus water to the pan.
  8. Step 8: Mix thoroughly and put the fried vegetable balls into the mixture.
  9. Step 9: Properly mix the balls along with the sauce.Garnish with spring onion and celery. Serve hot along with cooked rice.
