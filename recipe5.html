WHITE SAUCE PASTA

Ingredients:

  • semolina penne pasta
  • Vegetables
  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Heavy cream
  • Cheese
  • Herbs & Spices

Instructions:

  1. Step 1: In a large pot, bring the water to a boil. Then stir in the salt.
  2. Step 2: Add the dry pasta to the water, and stir a few times to prevent the pasta from sticking together.
  3. Step 3: Cook the pasta according to the package instructions (about 10-12 minutes), occasionally stirring, until al dente or softer according to your desired texture.
  4. Step 4: Once the pasta is cooked, reserve some starchy water for the sauce and drain the pasta in a colander over the sink. Set the cooked pasta aside.
  5. Step 5: Then add mixed veggies like carrot, bell pepper, broccoli, sweet corn, and salt.
  6. Step 6: Cook the veggies till they are tender but still crunchy, for around 2 minutes. Turn off the flame and keep it aside.
  7. Step 7:Slowly add in milk.Once the sauce is slightly thick, add the heavy cream, cheese, oregano, red chili flakes, black pepper, and salt.
  8. Step 8: Add the sauteed veggies and the cooked penne pasta to the sauce. Toss well to coat the pasta with creamy white sauce.
  9. Step 9: Lastly, transfer it to a serving plate and sprinkle some cheese and red chili flakes, if desired, and serve it immediately Enjoy!
