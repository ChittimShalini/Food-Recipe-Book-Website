SPICY EGG NOODLES

Ingredients:

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • ½ cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Instructions:

  1. Step 1: Stir flour and salt together in a large bowl. Add eggs, milk, and butter.
  2. Step 2: Knead dough until smooth, about 5 minutes. Let rest in a covered bowl for 10 minutes.
  3. Step 3: On a floured surface, roll dough out to a thickness of 1/8 to 1/4 inch. Cut into desired lengths and shapes.
  4. Step 4: Allow to air-dry before cooking, 10 to 15 minutes.
  5. Step 5: Cook noodles in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente, about 5 minutes.
