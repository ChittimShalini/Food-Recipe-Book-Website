GOLI VADA PAV

Ingredients:

  • large potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • green chilies, finely chopped
  • piece of ginger, grated
  • mustard seeds
  • cumin seeds
  • turmeric powderr
  • red chili powder
  • curry leaves, chopped
  • Salt,baking soda
  • oil
  • gram flour

Instructions:

  1. Step 1: Heat 1/2 tablespoon butter on a tawa over medium flame
  2. Step 2: Add 1/2 tablespoon dry garlic chutney over it.
  3. Step 3: Roast pav's inner side by placing it on tawa.
  4. Step 4: Press it a little and shallow fry until light brown: approx 20-30 second on medium flame.
  5. Step 5: Flip the pav and shallow fry outer sides as well until light brown.
  6. Step 6: Spread green chutney on inner side and place the vada inside pav.
  7. Step 7: Serve hot with tomato ketch up and green chutney.
